About Us

We are a team of three students from LPU – Kriti, Nitesh, and Pranav – passionate about computer science and problem-solving. This project is our attempt to simplify disk scheduling algorithms and help students visualize their working.

Disk Scheduling Algorithms

FCFS (First Come First Serve)

Requests are processed in the order they arrive.

SSTF (Shortest Seek Time First)

Chooses the request closest to the current head position.

SCAN (Elevator Algorithm)

Moves in one direction, then reverses at the end.

C-SCAN (Circular Elevator Algorithm)

Moves in one direction, then wraps around to the start.
